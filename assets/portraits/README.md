## Finding Suitable Images

Search for public domain Victorian portraits:
- Wikimedia Commons: "Victorian portrait photography 1880s"
- Library of Congress Digital Collections
- National Portrait Gallery (UK)
- Metropolitan Museum of Art (Open Access)

## Image Requirements

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 400x600 pixels or larger
- Style: Period-appropriate Victorian (1880s era)
- License: Public domain or CC0 preferred

The game will display colored placeholder portraits if images are not found.
